What is anime?

Anime is a japanese animated cartoon, animated film, or television entertainment

History

The first anime was created in 1917 when japanese animators expirinmented with animation. It was nammed "Katsudō Shashin". Over the 20th century anime gained a lot of populairity and it was globalized in the 1960 with the release of astro boy.
